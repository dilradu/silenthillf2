Silent Hill F is a fan-made continuation of the classic Silent Hill universe, set in the 1960s in the rural town of Ebisugaoka. The story follows a group of high school students who uncover dark secrets and face supernatural horrors that threaten their town. Players navigate eerie environments, encounter nightmarish creatures, and explore the psychological aspects of fear and suspense. The game emphasizes atmosphere, tension, and storytelling, staying true to the unsettling tone that the Silent Hill series is known for.
