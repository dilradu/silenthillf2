Shimizu Hinako

Hinako is the game’s protagonist: a shy, determined high school student from rural Ebisugaoka in the 1960s. Once cheerful as a child, she’s grown quiet and restrained under social pressure and family expectations. When fog and monstrous horrors overrun her town, she must fight to survive and discover her own strength.

深水雛子

Shu Iwai

Shu is Hinako’s childhood friend and ally. Raised in a family of physicians, he is knowledgeable about medicine and often serves as a calming, reliable presence. Though forward-thinking for his age, he shares deep bonds with Hinako and supports her through the terrors that engulf their town.

岩井修

Nishida Rinko

Rinko is another close friend of Hinako and Shu. She is known for being rule-abiding and a bit of a gossip, often creating tension in the group with her crush on Shu. Despite her chatter, she proves loyal and shows courage when faced with the horrors in Ebisugaoka.

西田凜子

Sakuko Igarashi

Sakuko is a gentle, dreamy friend whose family manages the local Shinto shrine. She offers spiritual insight and calm to the group, but beneath her soft demeanor lies a quiet courage that emerges when the town is overtaken by strange, supernatural forces.

五十嵐咲子

Kotoyuki Tsuneki

Kotoyuki, also known as the Fox Mask, is a mysterious figure connected to Hinako’s past and the supernatural events in Ebisugaoka. His intentions are enigmatic, often walking the line between protector and ominous presence, reflecting the eerie folklore of fox spirits.
